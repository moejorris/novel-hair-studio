The studio

A new story
in Raleigh

Novel Hair Studio opened in May 2025 with a simple idea: a boutique studio where every appointment feels personal, unhurried, and genuinely focused on you.

We specialize in blended color and lived-in cuts — the kind that look intentional at week one and effortless at week eight. Our services are named like chapters because that's how we think about hair: each visit is part of a longer, evolving story.

And yes — there's a book nook. Browse, borrow, or swap a read while you're here.

"Color that grows out beautifully.
Cuts that keep their shape."

The stylist

Meet Lianne

Lianne Padilla

Hairstylist · 14 Years Experience

Originally from New York, Lianne brings 14 years of expertise in blended color and effortless cuts to Raleigh. Her approach is intuitive — every appointment starts with a real conversation about what you want and how your hair actually behaves.

She is certified in Redken color, Keratin Complex treatments, and hair extensions, and she founded Novel Hair Studio in May 2025.

Lianne also curates the studio's book nook — a rotating collection for clients to browse, borrow, or swap during their visit.
